About Haridwar University


Haridwar University is a leading institution in Uttarakhand dedicated to innovation, research, and academic excellence. The university provides state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and an environment that encourages creativity and entrepreneurship among students.

The university hosts several technical events and national competitions to promote innovation and collaboration among students from across India. TechWizard 2.0 is one such initiative aimed at bringing together talented developers, designers, and innovators to solve real-world challenges.

Located in the spiritual city of Haridwar, the campus provides a perfect blend of modern education and cultural heritage, making it an ideal place for learning and innovation.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is TECHWIZARD 2.0?

TECHWIZARD 2.0 is a national-level hackathon where students collaborate to build innovative technology solutions within a limited time. Participants work on real-world problems, learn new technologies, and compete for exciting prizes.

2. How can I reach the hackathon venue?

The hackathon will be held at Haridwar University, Uttarakhand. Participants can reach the venue by train, bus, or personal transport. Detailed location and directions will be shared with registered teams before the event.

3. Who can participate?

Students from any recognized college or university who are interested in technology, development, and innovation can participate in the hackathon.

4. How much is the registration fee?

Registration for the hackathon is not free. Selected teams will be able to participate without any participation fee.

5. What’s the team size requirement?

Each team must have a minimum of 4 members and a maximum of 5 members(1 female member). Solo participation is not allowed.

6. What are the problem statements?

Problem statements will be based on domains like Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Cybersecurity, Healthcare Technology, Smart Cities, and Sustainability. Detailed themes will be announced before the hackathon.

7. What are the prizes?

The total prize pool is ₹93,000. Winners will receive cash prizes, certificates, and special recognition for their innovative solutions.

8. What’s included in the event?

Participants will get access to mentorship, networking opportunities, certificates, exciting challenges, and a chance to win prizes. Meals and basic facilities will also be provided during the event.

9. How do I register?

You can register through the official registration form available on the website. Simply click the “Register Now” button and fill in your team details.

10. Is there a code of conduct?

Yes. All participants are expected to maintain professionalism, respect fellow participants, and follow the hackathon rules. Any form of misconduct or plagiarism may lead to disqualification.

11. Where can I get updates?

All updates regarding the hackathon will be shared through the official website, email notifications, and social media channels of the event.

12. Whom can I contact for queries?

For any queries, you can contact the organizing team via email at tech.club.trojan@gmail.com or call the coordinators listed on the website.
