About The Event

What is TechWizard 2.0?

TechWizard 2.0 is the second edition of Haridwar University's flagship National Hackathon, powered by the Trojan Tech Club and Unstop. Bringing together the brightest student minds from across India, participants collaborate to solve real-world problems over an intense 36-hour coding marathon.

Teams of 4–5 members (with at least 1 female member mandatory) will compete across 3 rigorous rounds — PPT, Prototype, and Final Presentation — for a combined prize pool of ₹93,000.

👥 4–5 Members per Team
♀️ 1 Female Member Mandatory
📋 3 Rounds: PPT & Prototype
💰 Entry Fee: ₹1,500 per Team
📍 Haridwar University, Uttarakhand
📅 20 & 21 March 2026

Important

Hackathon Rules

01

Each team must consist of 4–6 members.

02

All team members must be registered participants.

03

All students and mentors must report to the auditorium by 9:00 AM sharp.

04

A minimum of 2 team members must be present at the desk at all times.

05

Leaving the premises is permitted only in emergencies with prior coordinator approval.

06

During presentation, a minimum of 2 team members must present their ideas.

07

Seeking help from students outside the premises for project development is strictly prohibited.

08

GitHub submission from every team member is mandatory.

09

Mentors must not write complete code for any team; violation leads to disqualification.
