About The Event

What is TechWizard 2.0?

TechWizard 2.0 is the second edition of Haridwar University's flagship National Hackathon, powered by the Trojan Tech Club and Unstop. Bringing together the brightest student minds from across India, participants collaborate to solve real-world problems over an intense 36-hour coding marathon.

Teams of 4–5 members (with at least 1 female member mandatory) will compete across 3 rigorous rounds — PPT, Prototype, and Final Presentation — for a combined prize pool of ₹93,000.

4–5 Members per Team
1 Female Member Mandatory
3 Rounds: PPT & Prototype
Entry Fee: ₹1,500 per Team
Haridwar University, Uttarakhand
26 & 27 March 2026

The Driving Force

About Trojan Club

Trojan Club is the leading student technical community of Roorkee College of Engineering, focused on building a strong culture of innovation, collaboration, and practical learning. Over the years, the club has grown into one of the most active and recognized student bodies on campus, organizing hackathons, coding competitions, workshops, and technical events that help students sharpen real-world skills.

With a vibrant network of 400+ active members, Trojan Club promotes peer-to-peer learning, industry interaction, and leadership development, creating opportunities for students to turn ideas into impact.
